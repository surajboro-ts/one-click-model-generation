## FP.0 Frequency picker

**Overview** 
The frequency picker widget allows users to select a specific time period to apply to an operation like syncing or scheduling. 
The widget has a variety of states for particular situations. 

**User Action**: The user can manipulate the frequency picker to allow them to set a specific time or date/s for an action to happen.

**System Response**: The frequency picker interface will adapt to user inputs to allow them to add a variety of levels of detail to their frequency request.
