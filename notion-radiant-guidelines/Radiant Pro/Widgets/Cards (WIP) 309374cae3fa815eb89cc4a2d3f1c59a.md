# Cards (WIP)

Cards contain content and actions about a single subject.

## Anatomy

The card container is the only required element in a card. All other elements shown here are optional. Card layouts can vary to support the types of content they contain.

**Additional actions**

Cards can support multiple actions, such as UI controls and an overflow menu. Because cards are entry points to more detailed information, they should contain a limited number of actions.

## Design spec

Cards have no singular layout, typographic, or image size. All cards should be designed to meet the needs of the content they present. This section shows a variety of card layouts to help showcase that variety.
